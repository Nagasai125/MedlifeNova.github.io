Service Learning Trip: Peru Spring 2020

An unforgettable journey of service, learning, and cultural immersion.

Trip Overview

Our Spring 2020 Service Learning Trip to Peru offered participants a unique opportunity to engage directly with local communities in the Sacred Valley region. Volunteers worked alongside community members on vital projects focusing on education and health infrastructure improvements.

Beyond the service work, the trip was designed as a deep cultural exchange, allowing participants to learn about Peruvian history, traditions, and daily life. It was a transformative experience for everyone involved, fostering global citizenship and a commitment to service.

Dates

March 15 - March 28, 2020

Location

Sacred Valley Region, Peru

Focus Areas

Education & Health Infrastructure

Trip Highlights & Activities

  • Collaborated on constructing a new classroom for a rural primary school.
  • Assisted in painting and refurbishing a local health clinic.
  • Participated in educational workshops with local students.
  • Experienced traditional Peruvian culture through homestays and local artisan visits.
  • Explored historical sites, including guided tours of Incan ruins.
