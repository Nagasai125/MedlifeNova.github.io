About Us

Our Chapter's Story and Commitment to Service.

The MEDLIFE NoVA High School Chapter, organized and led by President Aniketh Pondugula in collaboration with his Executive Board, has been instrumental in engaging high school students in meaningful community service initiatives, developing leadership skills, and creating lasting change for individuals living in poverty.

Under the leadership of Aniketh, the Chapter has hosted and participated in numerous impactful volunteer initiatives, including service at JK Farm, food preparation at DC Central Kitchen, local Red Cross blood drives, and various community outreach events. Additionally, the Chapter has actively engaged in fundraising efforts to support MEDLIFE’s global mission.

Committed to MEDLIFE’s mission, the Chapter continues to address the interconnected challenges of healthcare, education, and development by providing holistic support that empowers underserved communities to break the cycle of poverty.
